Preparation Tips

Smart ways to improve academic performance

📘 Study Planning

• Prepare a daily study timetable
• Study difficult subjects first
• Take short breaks to stay focused

⏱ Time Management

• Use Pomodoro technique
• Avoid distractions like mobile
• Set priorities for subjects

🧠 Subject-wise Strategy

• Programming: Practice daily
• Maths: Revise formulas & problems
• Theory: Prepare short notes

📝 Exam Preparation

• Solve previous question papers
• Take mock tests
• Identify weak areas early
